Welcome, friend.

On your screen lives the Zenscillator. There is no game; no goal; no objective here.

Simply click or tap to play the piano keys one at a time,
or use your computer's keyboard to play multiple notes simultaneously.

Click on the tabs below the piano ("sine", "triangle", "square", etc.)
to change the sound of the Zenscillator.

Hover over the "effects" panel to see which effects are available and activated.
Use your keyboard to toggle these effects and more.

Have fun playing your favorite melodies and observing the colors that soar across the screen!
